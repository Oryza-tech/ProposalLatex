This research focuses on optimizing the position accuracy of industrial AC servo
motor drive systems by implementing the \textit{S-Curve trajectory planning}
algorithm. The use of a \textit{dual-core microcontroller} architecture is
carried out to separate the numerical computation load of the trajectory from
the generation of precision control pulses. Comparative testing was conducted
against \textit{rectangular} and \textit{trapezoidal} profiles using the ED4270m
mechatronics module, with success parameters being the minimization of
mechanical oscillation and the effectiveness of \textit{settling time}. The
results of the study are expected to provide a reference for an industrial
motion control system that is reliable, low-cost, yet highly precise. \\[0.6cm]
